fyoosha the band

fyoosha is an original instrumental recording duo (guitar and drums).. a rock based melodic fusion of various contemporary music elements from rock, jazz, classical and blues with a touch of electronica.. a very original sound covering a full-spectrum sound, but not too far from the center.. thematically, fyoosha is an exploration and provocation of political rhetoric, set as lyrical epic journeys fueled by raging guitars, a fusion of music, imagery and historical sound clips.. the resolution is cheerful and ominous..Genre experimental instrumental Hometown toronto Record Label g spot
